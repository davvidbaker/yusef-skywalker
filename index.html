It is a period of great change on Tatooine. Young Yusef Skywalker is directing his last intergalactic fusion dance show before he graduates from space college, where he studies PPE: Podraces, Parsecs, and Economics.

 

Meanwhile, it is also a period of great civil war. All over the place. Huge space ships whizzing by, shooting at each other. Some look like little bow ties. We’ll call them bow tie fighters. And then there’s this huge fucking space ship called the Death Star. And the bad guys are flying this huge thing around like it’s no big deal, led by Darth motherfucking Vader -- the most badass guy to ever have terrible asthma.

 

So then the bad guys capture this princess with crazy buns in her hair, and she wears a metal bikini at some point. They blow up her planet, which is pretty fucking crazy if you think about it. The bad guys have the capability to blow up an ENTIRE PLANET. Prob seems like the good guys have no chance, right?

 

WRONG. Because Yusef is a good guy. And he’s the son of Darth Vader. WHAT?! I know. But he doesn’t know that yet. And there’s these people who are like superheroes but also kind of in a cult that teaches people how to move stuff with their minds. And Yusef is gonna drink the kool-aid, join the cult, and learn how to be awesome and kick his dad’s ASS and save the entire galaxy.

 

But first he has to learn about being awesome from this old motherfucker who used to teach Darth Vader. But Yusef doesn’t know that. Oh, and the princess and Yusef are brother and sister. AH! And he doesn’t know that either.

 

So what DOES Yusef know? Doesn’t matter, he’s the hero and it’s his mofuckin’ journey.

 

So Yusef learns to stop being terrible at everything and kicks his dad’s ass, as this whole war is going on. And there’s this old, green little dude who’s super calm and wise and stoned.

 

But back to the dance show! Right now Yusef’s directing and helping to choreograph this awesome dance show after being in this dance group for 7 OTHER INSANE SHOWS. And he’s gonna use a light saber I hope. Because that would be a fucking AWESOME DANCE. Oh, a light saber is this huge laser beam sword thing that somehow can cut through any substance. And people use it to fight. And DANCE?? We shall see.

 

Also Yusef doesn’t even care. He’s just doing his own thing, kicking ass, dancing, and not giving a fuck what gets in his way. Because he’ll dance around it, and/or use his fucking force powers on it. And he’ll do it all wearing a robe. Because that’s how real jedi’s do things. IN ROBES.

 

And all of Yusef’s friends are gonna come to the show and cheer and hoot and holler because Yusef is the man and is gonna fucking MOONWALK. I’m calling it now. He’s gonna moonwalk folks. I hope so. Don’t make a liar out of me, Yusef. Please do not fuck me on this.

 

Anyway, this is the prologue, so get ready for the real thing. We love you, Yusef. OH SHIT. I forgot about Han Solo. I wonder if he’ll be dancing. I hope so. I hear they got Harrison Ford to come back. He’s appearing in the dance. Doing a disco. It’s gonna be out of this WORLD.

 

So, that’s about it. Enjoy the show, and be super loud and cheer everyone on because Strictly Funk is awesome. Please keep your hands and feet off of the performers as they prepare to restore freedom to the galaxy. . .
